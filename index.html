Bhagat Singh

Biography

Bhagat Singh was born on 28 September 1907 in Punjab, British India. His family was deeply involved in the freedom struggle, which inspired him from a young age.

Quote

They may kill me, but they cannot kill my ideas. They can crush my body, but they will not be able to crush my spirit.
